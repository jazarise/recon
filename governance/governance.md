# Governance Model

ReconX operates under a structured governance model to ensure stability and longevity.

## Roles

1. **Core Maintainer:** Has push access to `main`. Approves major architectural shifts, manages releases, and handles security disclosures.
2. **Module Maintainer:** Owns a specific plugin or module (e.g. `modules/recon/nmap`). Approves PRs related to their domain.
3. **Reviewer:** Community members granted triage access to review incoming Pull Requests and test features.
4. **Contributor:** Anyone who submits a PR or issue to the repository.

## Expectations
Maintainers are expected to review PRs within 7 days. Contributors must adhere to the `CONTRIBUTING.md` standards.
